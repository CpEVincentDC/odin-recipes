Pork Menudo Recipe


Filipino | Kid-friendly | Entrant

A Pork Menudo (stew)

Menudo, also known as ginamay or ginagmay

A traditional Stew from the Philippines made with pork and sliced liver in tomato sauce with carrots and potatoes

Ingredients

This dish has been optimized for 3 servings. Quantity and times may need to be varied.

Our best tips & tricks for customized portion size If the quantities are increased, the cooking time may be extended, so look again. Use water & spices sparingly at first and add more later if needed.

Recipe Instruction

    Step 1

  1. Sauté potato and carrot in 1 tbsp oil until golden brown. Remove and set aside for 10 minutes.

    2. Step 2

  2. Season pork shoulder with 1 sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®. Sauté pork in the same pan. Remove and set aside for 5 minutes.

    3. Step 3

  3. Sauté garlic, onion and bell peppers. Pour tomato sauce and water. Add pork, bay leaf, pepper and sugar. Simmer for 45 minutes.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add potato and carrot. Season with remaining sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap® and simmer for another 10 minutes. Transfer into a serving plate and serve.
